There were disturbing levels of activation during the last access into the Booster.  This has caused some worries, particularly in the RF group.  To initially address this problem, I've tried to identify problem areas, and looked at their typical loss levels in order to arrive at some new limits.  I've used data from our standard Booster surveys, and selected the following dates:
  • 8/28/03 - Just before MiniBooNE started running in earnest
  • 4/10/03  and 4/29/03 - Corresponding to some of our best running
  • 6/17/03 - Our most recent access
All of these surveys had similar cooldown times, and I haven't applied any cooldown correction.  I've corrected all data to mR/hr@1f as follows:
  • 1ft measurements - no correction.
  • Contact measurements EXCEPT short straights - divide by 5.
  • Contact measurements at short straights - divide by 2.  This is because the contact point is on the stand, not on the beampipe itself.
