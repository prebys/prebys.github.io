Guidelines for talks:
  • Each talk should be NO LONGER than 10 MINUTES! 
  • This means 4-6 slides (not including title slide) depending on how much you talk per slide. 
  • A decent talk might be organized as follows:
    •   It is good to begin the talk by saying what your project is, and why it is important (i.e. context in the field of accelerator science) - slide 1. 
    • Then, state what the project goals are, and your particular role in meeting those goals - slide 2. 
    • *Briefly* describe how you worked to meet those goals - slide 3,
    • and what results you had - slide 4.
